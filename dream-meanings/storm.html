What does it mean to dream about storms?

A storm in a dream usually reflects emotional turmoil, conflict, or a sense that trouble is building. Thunder, lightning, and wind mirror feelings that feel powerful and beyond your control.

An approaching storm can point to anxiety about something on the horizon; being caught in one may reflect being in the middle of a hard time. The calm after the storm can be a hopeful sign that a difficult period is passing.

Ask: what storm is brewing in my life, and what would help me weather it?
