What does it mean to dream about being cheated on?

Dreaming that a partner is cheating rarely means it's actually happening. Much more often it reflects your own insecurities, fears of not being enough, or trust that's been shaken somewhere in your life.

It can surface during stressful times, after a betrayal (even by someone else), or when you feel neglected or unsure where you stand. Sometimes the 'cheating' is really about feeling replaced or overlooked — at work, in a friendship, or in the relationship itself.

A gentler question than 'is it real?' is: where do I feel insecure or unseen right now, and what would help me feel safe?
