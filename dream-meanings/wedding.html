What does it mean to dream about weddings?

A wedding in a dream often symbolizes union, commitment, and the joining of two parts — whether two people, or two sides of yourself coming together.

It can reflect real feelings about a relationship or a milestone, but just as often it's about integration: merging different parts of who you are, or committing to a new path. A stressful or wrong wedding may mirror doubts about a commitment or a decision.

Ask: what am I committing to right now, and does it feel like a true union or a forced one?
