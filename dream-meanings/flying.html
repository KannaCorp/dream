What does it mean to dream about flying?

Flying is one of the most positive dreams to have. It often reflects freedom, rising above a problem, a burst of confidence, or a wish to escape something that's been weighing on you.

Soaring easily can mean you feel in control and hopeful. Struggling to stay up, or flying low and bumping into things, can point to obstacles or self-doubt getting in the way of something you want. Either way, flying usually signals a desire to break free and see the bigger picture.
