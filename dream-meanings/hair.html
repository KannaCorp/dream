What does it mean to dream about hair falling out?

Dreaming that your hair falls out often relates to confidence, identity, and worries about how you appear to others. Hair is closely tied to self-image, so losing it can mirror a fear of losing your appeal, strength, or control.

It can surface during stressful times, aging worries, or periods when you feel less like yourself. Cutting your own hair in a dream, by contrast, can reflect a fresh start or letting go of the past.

Ask: what part of my identity or confidence feels under threat right now?
