What does it mean to dream about being naked in public?

Dreaming that you're naked in public is a classic anxiety dream. It usually reflects feeling exposed, vulnerable, or afraid that others will see your flaws or 'the real you.'

It often appears before something that makes you feel judged — a presentation, a new job, a social situation. Sometimes no one in the dream even notices, which can be your mind reassuring you that your fears are bigger than the reality.

Ask: where do I feel exposed right now, and is anyone really judging me as harshly as I fear?
