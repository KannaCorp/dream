Frequently asked questions

What does it mean to dream about falling?

Dreaming of falling often points to a loss of control, insecurity, or a fear of failing at something that matters to you. In the app you can look up falling and thousands of other symbols, or describe your exact dream for a personal AI reading.

What does it mean to dream about your teeth falling out?

It's one of the most common dreams. It usually reflects anxiety about how others see you, a fear of losing control, or a big change you feel unprepared for.

Is there a free dream interpretation app?

Yes. Dream Interpreter & AI is available free on both the App Store (iPhone) and Google Play (Android). It includes an A–Z dream dictionary, AI interpretation of your own dreams, and a dream journal.

How does the AI dream interpretation work?

You describe your dream in your own words and the AI reads the symbols and how they may fit your life, in seconds. It's for reflection and self-insight — not fortune-telling or professional advice.

What languages does it support?

The app is available in English, German, Spanish, French, Italian, Portuguese, Russian and Serbian.
